Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Wallframe Analytics ("the App") accesses Pinterest API data solely for analytics purposes. The App reads public Pin and Board metadata (impressions, saves, clicks) belonging to the authenticated Wallframe Pinterest account.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used exclusively to generate performance reports for the Wallframe brand. No personal user data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Analytics data is stored locally and not shared with any external service. No cookies or tracking technologies are used.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Pinterest API under Pinterest's Developer Terms. No other third-party services are integrated.
