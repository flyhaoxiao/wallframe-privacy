Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Wallframe ("we", "our", "us") operates a Pinterest API integration used solely to manage and publish content to our own Pinterest business account (pinterest.com/flyhaoxiao). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in connection with that integration.

1. Scope

This tool is for single-creator, single-account use only. It is not a multi-user platform and is not distributed to any third parties. Only the account owner (flyhaoxiao@gmail.com) operates this integration.

2. Information Accessed

We access only our own Pinterest account data via Pinterest's official OAuth 2.0 API. This includes account profile information and the ability to create and manage Pins and Boards on our own account. We do not access, collect, or process data from any other Pinterest users.

3. How We Use Information

Pinterest API access tokens are used exclusively to publish Pins (product images, titles, descriptions, and links) to our own Pinterest boards. No data is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than publishing our own content.

4. Data Storage

We do not store Pinterest user data on any external server. Authentication tokens are stored locally on the operator's personal machine and are never transmitted to third-party services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer any data to third parties under any circumstances.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the integration. They can be revoked at any time from the Pinterest account settings.

7. Your Rights

As this tool operates solely on our own account, no third-party user rights apply. If you have questions about data practices, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
